ABOUT MOI!

Dana is a 20 year old girl from San Diego who took up crotcheting on a whim last winter break. Her grandmother used to make hats for her birthday each year and she wanted to return the favor! Dana's crotchet designs gained so much popularilty among her friends and family over the year that it was time to take her business to the next level and CROTCHET-NA was born. All pieces are handmade with love and care!
